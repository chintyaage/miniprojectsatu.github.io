The Jewel of the Amalfi Coast

From the moment you catch your first glimpse of the town's iconic church spire and multi-colored houses clinging to the cliffs above the sparkling Mediterranean, you'll be enchanted by Positano's allure. Wander through the narrow, serpentine streets, where bougainvillea-draped staircases lead you past boutiques, art galleries, and family-owned trattorias serving up the region's renowned cuisine.

The Impact of Overtourism in Venice, Italy

Venice, the iconic Italian city built on the water, has been grappling with the challenges of overtourism for several years now. As one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world, Venice has seen a surge in visitor numbers that has put immense strain on the city's infrastructure and fragile ecosystem.
